# Svea Solar — Fullstack Engineer Job Description

> Saved for interview preparation reference.

---

## Company

At Svea Solar, we have a clear vision - to fix the planet. As Sweden's and one of Europe's leading players in energy technology, we are driving the energy transition with solar power and smart energy solutions. ⚡

## Role: Fullstack Engineer — Website Team

As our Fullstack Engineer in the Website team, you'll be part of a talented and ambitious agile team. Together you'll design and implement technical solutions for Svea Solar's website and related areas.

You'll work closely with a Tech lead, a frontend engineer, an analyst, and a product manager, reporting to our Engineering Manager Anna. We follow a collaboration-first approach, working three days a week from our Stockholm office.

As a Fullstack Developer, you'll have real impact on a key part of our business—our website drives leads and sales. By building tools that increase sales, you'll also amplify our positive impact, helping Svea Solar move closer to our mission of ridding the planet of fossil fuels—all while growing your skills in a friendly, collaborative culture.

## Key Responsibilities

- Build and maintain both frontend and backend features
- Develop modern, accessible, and semantically correct websites
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-structured code
- Design and implement REST APIs and integrations with our CMS written in Python
- Collaborate with designers, product managers, and fellow engineers to create functional and user-friendly solutions
- Contribute to architectural discussions and best practices, supporting long-term scalability and event-driven design

## Requirements

- Value team success over personal success and thrive in a collaborative dynamic environment
- Have at least 5 years of professional experience in software development, including at least 2 years in backend
- Have strong experience with: Python, REST APIs, and event-driven design
- Are fluent in English and/or Swedish

## Bonus Points

- Comfortable with Django or Wagtail
- TypeScript, React, Node.js, Express
- Have worked with one or several CMS and have experience of SEO
- Solid knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, and SASS

## Interview Focus Areas (mentioned)

1. Architectural choices
2. Reasoning around implementation choices

## Benefits

- Impact: Work directly contributes to fixing the planet and driving the energy transition
- Value-driven workplace
- Ownership: Grow, take responsibility, and power up
- Diversity and inclusivity
- Occupational pension, 30 days vacation, wellness allowance, parking
- Hybrid work: up to two days a week from home
